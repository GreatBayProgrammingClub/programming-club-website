Activities

What we do

AppDevelopment

The AppDevelopment activity challenges students to develop apps as a team. These apps are ranging from games, mobile, web, to console app.

Talk

Talk is a presentation by a student about a particular computer science topic. This is an opportunity for students to share their skills.

Meetup

Meetup is for building Great Bay Community College’s computer science community. It is where we share our skills and talk about computer science.
